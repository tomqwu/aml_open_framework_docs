Exhibit 5 The thesis

One specification serves three reviewers — without re-translation.

A single machine-readable file is authored by Compliance, executed unchanged by Engineering, and replayed byte-for-byte by an Auditor. Three different readings of the same artifact — every quarter, every regulator visit.

Source of truth
aml.yaml
written once · committed to version control
First line · Compliance
The risk policy in machine-readable form, in our own language.
"Our scenarios, our thresholds, every line cited to a regulation."
Engineering
The schema we build from. The data the engine consumes.
"Compiles deterministically — same spec, same data, same answer."
Auditor · 2LoD
The artifact I can replay any quarter, byte-for-byte.
"When the regulator asks 'who decided?' — the answer is in the file."
Key takeaway Eliminating the translation layer between three lines of defence removes the most-cited finding in 2024–26 consent orders: "controls existed; the bank could not evidence they were used."
