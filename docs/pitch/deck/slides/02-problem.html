Policy drift

"We updated the policy in March. Did anyone update the rules?"
SYMPTOM 02

Black-box rules

Compliance can't read SQL. Engineering can't read FATF Recommendations. The bridge is one senior who's been here ten years.

"What does ml_risk_scorer_v3 actually check?"

Audit panic

Examiner asks "show me where this rule traces to PCMLTFR." Three weeks of archaeology, four Slack threads, one panicked spreadsheet.

"Can someone find the link to the regulation again?"
