Escalations stop drowning
in unread email.

01
Cron'd into your channel. Every 4-24h, a rendered digest of escalated cases, breached SLAs, and newly-aging queues drops into Slack or Teams.
02
Suppressed when nothing fires. --suppress-empty skips the post when the window is clean — channels stay high-signal.
03
Webhook-only. No vendor connector, no SaaS forwarder. Slack/Teams incoming-webhook URLs in env vars, one egress destination, your perimeter.
The supervisor finds out about a breach when it happens, not at end-of-day. That alone is most of the "why we keep adding tools" problem solved.
