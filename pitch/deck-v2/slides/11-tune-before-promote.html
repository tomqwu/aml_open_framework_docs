Act IV · The Detection Substrate

MLRO challenges the model.
Without a six-week vendor study.

01
Backtester replays last quarter's data against a candidate threshold change. Returns precision / recall / alert delta — the exact evidence SR 26-2 and OSFI E-23 ask for.
02
Tune the spec, not the SQL. The tuning grid lives in the rule itself, alongside the regulation citation. Grid sweep is one command; outputs a YAML patch you can hand to 1LoD.
03
Then propose-change. The delta becomes the rationale paragraph in the 2LoD packet. The packet attaches to the spec PR. Same loop, every threshold, every quarter.
Model challenge stops being a vendor engagement. It becomes a Tuesday afternoon.
