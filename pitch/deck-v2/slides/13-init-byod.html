Zero to day-one detection —
before the kettle boils.

01
Pick an archetype. Schedule-I bank · community bank · VASP · fintech. Each scaffolds with sensible contracts, three starter rules, four queues, and a STR form pre-cited.
02
Map your warehouse. aml byod inspects your CSV/Parquet/DuckDB schemas and proposes the column mapping with confidence scores. Low-confidence rows are flagged, not auto-decided.
03
Validate, then run. aml validate-data checks completeness against your spec — every column, every quality check, before the engine touches a row.
Most pilots stall on "where do we even start?". This is the path from fresh repo to a regulator-mappable spec running on real data — same afternoon.
