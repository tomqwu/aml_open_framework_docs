Act III · The Daily Ritual

Threshold changes leave the email thread.
They join the audit trail.

01
1LoD proposes. One command produces a 6-section packet — rationale, expected impact, full diff, citations, and a sign-off block.
02
2LoD reviews in-line. The packet attaches to the spec PR. Review happens against the diff, in GitHub — not in Outlook, not in Word.
03
MLRO countersigns. Signed packet + commit history together are the audit-trail artifact. Eighteen months from now, the answer to "who decided?" is not in someone's inbox.
SR 26-2 / OSFI E-23 / FCA CASS expect the same thing: an evidenced governance ritual. This is what one looks like.
