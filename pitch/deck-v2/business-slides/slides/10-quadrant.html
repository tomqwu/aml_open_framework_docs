Strategic Planning Assumption
By April 2026, SR 26-2 folds AML transaction monitoring into model-risk inventory — every ML-first vendor carries a model-validation tax on every typology.
AML Open Framework — Visionary, by design
Spec-as-policy is not a model under SR 26-2. Hash-chained replay; multi-jurisdiction templating; ISO 20022 in-tree. Buyer that lands: 2LoD MRM / Audit at any Cat-2+ bank.
Veteran platforms — Leaders by inertia
Win Tier-1 RFPs by default. Differentiator: scale + audit comfort. Where we slot: 2LoD challenger model for SR 26-2 independent re-implementation.
AI-native challengers — Visionary but ML-taxed
Hawk:AI Series C ($56M+$10M); ComplyAdvantage cites 65–85% FP reduction. Adjacent, not opposed — they win mid-market; we cover the same buyer only when the buyer wants source-available code.
Key takeaway The framework doesn't compete with veteran platforms on detection scale — it competes on provability. Under SR 26-2, that is now the buyer's procurement criterion.
